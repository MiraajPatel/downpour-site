How it works

Four steps. One invoice — after you win.

STEP 01

We map your niche and screen the fit

We pick one lane, define the exact buyer, and pressure-test whether your motion actually closes meetings. If the math doesn't work for both of us, we tell you before we start — not after.

STEP 02

We build a sending engine off your main domain

We register and warm dedicated look-alike domains (get-yourbrand.com, try-yourbrand.com) with full SPF, DKIM, and DMARC. Your primary domain never sends a cold email. Replies still land in your inbox, from your brand.

STEP 03

We run hand-personalized, AI-augmented outbound

Human-written, research-led campaigns — not a bot spraying templates. We coordinate email with your founder LinkedIn presence, keep spam complaints under 0.3%, and book qualified meetings straight onto your calendar.

STEP 04

You close. Then — and only then — we invoice 5%

Every prospect is tracked to your CRM before we send. When a tracked deal closes and you're paid, we bill 5% of the contract value. Net-30. That's the entire transaction.

Why founders trust us

Why founders trust Downpour with their pipeline

Paid on close, not on promises

Our incentive is your revenue, full stop. We don't get rich booking low-intent meetings or billing a monthly retainer. If you don't close, we don't earn — so we only chase deals that actually convert.

We protect your primary domain

Cold volume never touches your main domain. We warm dedicated sending domains for 21 days, cap sends at 20–40 per mailbox a day, and hold bounces under 2%. Your deliverability stays clean.

Sent from your own brand

Prospects see your company, your founder, your voice — not a faceless agency. Replies route to your inbox. Buyers meet you, warmed by a known-sender lift of 3–5x over anonymous outreach.

AI-augmented, human-run

AI does the research and the heavy lifting. Humans write every message and read every reply. The "fully autonomous SDR" story collapsed in 2025 — we run the model that actually books meetings.

Attribution settled before we send

Unique tracking domains, UTMs into your CRM, a written attribution window, and shared pipeline visibility with audit rights. You always know which meetings and deals came from us — no arguments at invoice time.

One niche, done properly

We don't serve every B2B category at once. We go deep on a small set of lanes — CPG-into-retail brands with a regulatory buying trigger, and global founders selling into the US — so our playbook compounds for you.
